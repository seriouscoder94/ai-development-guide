Real Examples of AI Interactions

Learn from practical examples of good and bad AI communication

Writing Assistance

Poor Prompt:

❌ "Make my writing better"

Why it's poor: No context, no specific requirements, and no sample text provided.

Effective Prompt:

✅ "Please help me make this product description more engaging for our e-commerce website. The target audience is tech-savvy professionals aged 25-40. Here's the current text: [your text]. Please focus on highlighting the benefits rather than just features."

Why it works: Provides context, target audience, current text, and specific improvement goals.

Technical Problem Solving

Poor Prompt:

❌ "Why isn't my code working?"

Why it's poor: No code provided, no error message, no context about what the code should do.

Effective Prompt:

✅ "I'm trying to create a function that calculates the average of numbers in an array. Here's my current code: [code]. When I input an empty array, it returns NaN instead of 0. How can I fix this?"

Why it works: Includes the code, the specific issue, what's been tried, and what the expected behavior is.

Data Analysis

Poor Prompt:

❌ "Analyze this data"

Why it's poor: No specific analysis goals, no context about what insights are needed.

Effective Prompt:

✅ "I have sales data from our online store for the past 6 months. Can you help me identify: 1) The top-selling products by revenue, 2) Any seasonal trends in sales, and 3) Which marketing campaigns had the highest conversion rates? Here's the data: [data]"

Why it works: Clear objectives, specific metrics to analyze, and context about the data.
